Tougher Times

Common Item

Description:

+15% (+15% per stack) chance to block incoming damage. Unaffected by luck. Corrupted by Safer Spaces.

Strategy:

Tougher Times stacks hyperbolically, meaning that each Tougher Times gives you less stacking bonus for each stack you have.

It's best not to invest too heavily into Tougher Times unless you have no other options, the diminishing returns granted by stacking Tougher Times lend it to being less efficient than other defensive options like Oddly-shaped Opal.

It may be worth using a Pluripotent Larva to corrupt your Tougher Times into a Safer Spaces if you have enough corruptable items to justify sacrificing the extra life, generally you want to do this before picking up any Boss Items as not to corrupt them into Newly Hatched Zoea.

Notes:

Tougher Times can block any incoming source of damage except Void Fog and Strong Void Fog, this includes self inflicted damage and fall damage.

Void Reaver implosions cannot be blocked by Tougher Times.

Tougher Times provides a base chance of 15% to block damage, but that 15% is run through this calculation f(x) = (1 - (1 / .15 + 1)) which results in one stack of Tougher Times actually providing a 13% chance to block damage.

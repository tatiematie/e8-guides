Tougher Times

Common Item

Description:

15% (+15% per stack) chance to block incoming damage. Unaffected by luck. Corrupted by Safer Spaces .

Strategy:

Tougher Times  stacks hyperbolically, meaning that each Tougher Times  gives you less stacking bonus for each stack you have.

If you're going to keep any Tougher Times , it's best to keep just one if possible. Other defensive options like Oddly-shaped Opal  and Safer Spaces  are more efficient uses of your resources.

Notes:

Tougher Times  can block any incoming source of damage except for Void Fog  and Strong Void Fog , this includes self inflicted damage, fall damage, and tick damage.

Void Reaver  implosions cannot be blocked by Tougher Times .

Tougher Times  actually provides a 13% chance to block incoming damage in spite of what it's Logbook entry says.

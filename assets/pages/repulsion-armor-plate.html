Repulsion Armor Plate

Common Item

Description:

Reduce incoming damage by 5 (+5 per stack), damage cannot be reduced below 1.

Strategy:

Repulsion Armor Plate prevents you from gaining Curse stacks from sources of chip damage such as Lesser Wisp shots and Stone Titan lasers.

Stacking Repulsion Armor Plate wastes resources, scrap existing Repulsion Armor Plates before picking up new ones if possible.

Repulsion Armor Plate prevents gaining Curse stacks from charging Blood Pillars, otherwise it is almost impossible to maintain One-Shot Protection when going to Mithrix without a Rose Buckler or Jade Elephant.

Synergy:

Gain easy Gold in the early stages by pairing Repulsion Armor Plate with Roll of Pennies and tanking a few Lesser Wisp shots or burns from Fire Elites.

Notes:

Taking a Repulsion Armor Plate to Mithrix results in him giving your items back out of order during Phase 4 of the boss fight (PC & console).

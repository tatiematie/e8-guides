Repulsion Armor Plate

Common Item

Description:

Reduce incoming damage by 5 (+5 per stack). Cannot be reduced below 1.

Strategy:

Having a Repulsion Armor Plate  is recommended as it prevents accumulating Curse  stacks from chip damage (Lesser Wisps , Overloading  orbs, etc.).

Picking up more than one Repulsion Armor Plate  is a waste of resources. Scrapping existing Repulsion Armor Plates  before picking up new ones allows you to print other items that scale defense better, such as Oddly-shaped Opal  and Tougher Times .

Repulsion Armor Plate  can also be used to prevent taking Curse  stacks from charging Lunar Blood Pillars, preserving your One-Shot Protection for the boss fight.

Synergy:

You can earn gold quickly by pairing Repulsion Armor Plate  with Roll of Pennies  and taking shots from Lesser Wisps or Fire Elites .

Notes:

Having Repulsion Armor Plate  results in Mithrix giving your items back in a different order than he took them from you in Phase 4 (console & PC).

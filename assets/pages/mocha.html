Mocha

Common Item

Description:

Increases attack speed by 7.5% (+7.5% per stack) and movement speed by 7% (+7% per stack).

Strategy:

In spite of being half as effective, Mocha  can be a decent alternative to true movement speed and attack speed items like Paul's Goat Hoof  and Solier's Syringe .

It is more efficient to build into real movement speed or attack speed if provided the opportunity.

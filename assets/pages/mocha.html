Mocha

Common Item

Description:

Gain 7.5% (+7.5% per stack) attack speed and 7% (+7% per stack) movement speed.

Strategy:

Though 1/2 as effective as Paul's Goat Hoof and Soldier's Syringe, it can be beneficial to hold large quantities of Mocha if you find yourself in a situation where you need to print Common Items from a 3D Printer without any White Scrap available.

In this situation, you're only risking 1/2 of a full speed item if you lose a Mocha rather than a Paul's Goat Hoof or Soldier's Syringe, 1/3 in the case of Energy Drink.

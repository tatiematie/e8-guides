Tri-Tip Dagger

Common Item

Description:

Gain a 10% (+10% per stack) chance to bleed the target for 240% base damage. Corrupted by Needletick.

Strategy:

Survivors with high attack speed and high proc coefficients make the best use of Tri Tip Dagger, survivors with shotgun skills can also apply bleed decently well.

Melee and skill-based survivors do not benefit from bleed without the help of other items like Plasma Shrimp or Polylute, but they still won't stack bleed as efficiently Commando or MUL-T, for example.

Synergy:

Not all survivors can make effective use of Tri-Tip Dagger, but The Crowdfunder provides an out for survivors who didn't end up collecting items that suit their skills.

The Crowdfunder has a 1.0 proc coefficient and fires at rates that rival that of Commando's primary attack, even 1-2 Tri-Tip Daggers can allow survivors to stack bleed as long as they have enough Gold stockpiled.

Notes:

Bleed's duration depends on the proc coefficient of the attack that procced it. Bleed has a base duration of 3s, which is multiplied by the proc coefficient (3s * 1.0 = 3s).

Topaz Brooch

Common Item

Description:

Gain a temporary barrier on kill for 15 health (+15 per stack).

Strategy:

Topaz Brooch  is helpful during the first couple stages of a run but it's effectiveness drops as enemies begin to deal more damage.

It's best to scrap Topaz Brooches  unless you have multiple or are gathering on-kill effects to use Forgive Me Please , White Scrap  is more useful in the long run.

Synergy:

Using Forgive Me Please  will trigger Topaz Brooch , but to recieve a steady amount of barrier you will also need 1-2 Fuel Cells  and a Soulbound Catalyst .

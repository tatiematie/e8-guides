Topaz Brooch

Common Item

Description:

On kill, gain temporary barrier equal to 15hp (+15 per stack).

Strategy:

Topaz Brooch can be helpful early in a run if paired with area-of-effect items like Gasoline or Will o' the Wisp, but its effectiveness diminishes as enemies begin to deal more damage.

Topaz Brooch is better off scrapped for White Scrap as that will be more beneficial throughout your run unless you're collecting Forgive Me Please synergies.
